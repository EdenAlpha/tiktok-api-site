Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to InboxHub for TikTok ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our unified inbox management service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you register, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Name (if provided)
  • Account preferences and settings

2.2 TikTok Account Data

To provide our unified inbox service, we collect:

  • TikTok access tokens (encrypted)
  • TikTok usernames and profile information
  • Message content from your connected accounts
  • Follower counts and engagement metrics

2.3 Technical Information

We automatically collect:

  • IP address and browser type
  • Device information
  • Usage patterns and service analytics

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide and maintain the unified inbox service
  • Sync messages across your connected TikTok accounts
  • Display message threads and conversation history
  • Improve our service and develop new features
  • Communicate important service updates
  • Ensure security and prevent abuse
We do not: Sell your data, read your private messages for advertising, or share your content with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Encryption

All sensitive data, including TikTok access tokens, is encrypted using industry-standard AES-256 encryption both at rest and in transit.

4.2 Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • Account data: Until you delete your account
  • Message content: Synchronized in real-time but not permanently stored
  • Backup data: Encrypted and automatically deleted after 30 days

4.3 Security Measures

We implement:

  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Two-factor authentication for all administrative access
  • Secure API connections to TikTok's platform
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted vendors who help us operate our service (e.g., hosting providers)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger or acquisition

We never sell your personal data or message content to third parties.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access and download your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export your message history

6.2 Account Settings

Through your account dashboard, you can:

  • Disconnect TikTok accounts at any time
  • Adjust notification preferences
  • Manage connected devices and sessions

7. Third-Party Services

7.1 TikTok API

Our service integrates with TikTok's API. Your use of our service is subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service. We only access data necessary to provide the unified inbox functionality.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we learn we have collected data from a child, we will delete it immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by email or through our service. Your continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
